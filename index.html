CSIndex provides meaningful and transparent data about Brazilian scientific production in Computer Science. Currently, we focus on full research papers published in the main track of selected conferences. The papers are retrieved from DBLP. See our FAQ for details or this short talk (in Portuguese). To report missing papers or other questions, use this form or open an issue on GitHub. You can also follow us on Twitter.
